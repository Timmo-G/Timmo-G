### 🏛️ About Me | 关于我
I am a developer with a background in **Architecture**, pivoting towards the realm of **Computer Graphics**. I leverage my strong spatial reasoning and aesthetic sense to explore the frontiers of visual computing.

我拥有深厚的**建筑学背景**，正致力于探索**计算机图形学**的边界。我将空间思维与算法逻辑结合，专注于构建数字世界的视觉体验。
